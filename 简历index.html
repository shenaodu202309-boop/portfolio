申澳芏 Aodu Shen

我是申澳芏，一名具备完整项目执行能力的独立动画创作者，专注于二维动画、三维动画与定格动画。从前期概念开发、角色与场景设计、分镜和动态分镜，到动画制作与最终交付，我能够独立推进完整创作流程，也乐于在团队中协作。我擅长根据项目需求灵活运用不同的视觉风格与制作技术，在保证叙事清晰和执行效率的同时，为作品建立鲜明的视觉语言与情感表达。
My name is Aodu Shen, an independent animator with end-to-end production experience across 2D, 3D, and stop-motion animation. I can take a project from concept development, character and environment design, storyboarding, and animatics through animation production and final delivery, while also collaborating effectively within a team. I adapt visual styles and techniques to each brief, balancing clear storytelling and efficient execution with a distinctive visual language and emotional impact.
