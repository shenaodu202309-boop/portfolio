我是申澳芏，一名独立动画创作者，专注于二维动画、三维动画和定格动画的创作与制作。我的作品涵盖了从概念设计、分镜制作到最终动画呈现的完整流程。我热衷于探索不同的动画风格和技术，致力于通过视觉叙事传达独特的故事和情感。

My name is Aodu Shen, an independent animator specialising in 2D animation, 3D animation, and stop-motion animation. I am involved in every stage of the creative process, from concept design and storyboarding to animation production and final delivery. Through my work, I enjoy experimenting with different visual styles, techniques, and forms of storytelling, aiming to create distinctive and emotionally engaging animated experiences.
